# Varnam Aesthetics: Website Walkthrough

Welcome to your new Varnam Aesthetics website! This guide will help you understand how your customers will use your website to browse and buy your beautiful ornaments, and how you can manage everything smoothly behind the scenes.

## 1. How Your Customers Use the Website

Your website is designed to be very easy and friendly for customers to use. 

* **Browsing Products**: Customers can click the "Shop" link at the top or on their mobile menu to view your entire collection. They can see high-quality images and the prices clearly labeled.
* **Adding to Cart**: When a customer wants to buy something, they simply click "Add to Cart." A little red bubble on your shopping cart icon will automatically update so they know their item was saved.
* **Checking Out**: Once they are ready, they click the cart and proceed to Checkout. Here, they will quickly fill out their Name, Email, and Delivery Address.

## 2. The Simple Payment Process (No Extra Fees)

We have built a seamless, zero-fee payment process using direct UPI payments.

* When checking out, customers will see your unique **QR Code** and your official **UPI ID** (`kondapallisirihamsini9@okhdfcbank`).
* If they are browsing on their mobile phones, they will see instant "Pay with GPay" and "Pay with PhonePe" buttons. Tapping these will automatically open their payment app with the exact price and your UPI details pre-filled.
* Once they send the money, the customer takes a quick screenshot of the success page and uploads it on your checkout page right before placing the order.

## 3. How You Will Get Notified

You won't miss a single sale or customer question! 

* **New Orders**: Every time a customer places an order, you will instantly receive an email at **`Kondapallisirihamsini9@gmail.com`**. This email contains their delivery address, a list of what they bought, and the total price.
* **Custom Designs**: If a customer fills out the "Request a Custom Design" form and uploads inspiration pictures, all of their details and images will also be emailed directly to your inbox.

## 4. The Admin Dashboard (Managing Your Store)

At the very bottom of your website footer under "Quick Links", there is an inconspicuous link called **Admin Portal**. 

If you log in to this portal using your Admin email (`admin@varnamaesthetics.com`) and your secret password (`password`):
* You can easily view all the recent orders that have come in.
* You can check the payment screenshots to verify that you received the money.
* You can instantly add new earrings or products to your shop. Just type the product name, set a price, upload a picture, and it immediately appears on your public "Shop" page!
* You can delete old products when you run out of stock.
