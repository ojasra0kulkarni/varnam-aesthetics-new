Varnam Aesthetics: Website Walkthrough

Welcome to your completely custom Varnam Aesthetics website! This very simple guide will help you understand exactly how your customers will use your website to browse and buy your beautiful ornaments, and how you can manage everything smoothly behind the scenes.

1. How Your Customers Use the Website

Your website is designed to be incredibly easy and friendly for customers to use.

  • Browsing Products: Customers can click the "Shop" link at the top or on their mobile menu to view your entire collection. They can see high-quality images and the prices clearly labeled.
  • Adding to Cart: When a customer wants to buy something, they simply click "Add to Cart." A little red bubble on your shopping cart icon will automatically update so they know their item was saved.
  • Checking Out: Once they are ready, they click the cart and proceed to Checkout. Here, they will easily fill out their Name, Email, and Delivery Address.

2. The Simple Payment Process (No Extra Fees)

We built a seamless payment process using direct UPI so you don't have to pay any middleman transaction fees.

  • When checking out, customers will see your unique QR Code and your official UPI ID (kondapallisirihamsini9@okhdfcbank).
  • If they are ordering from their phones, they will see instant "Pay with GPay" and "Pay with PhonePe" buttons. Tapping these will instantly open their payment app with the exact total price and your UPI details automatically filled in for them!
  • Once they send the payment, the customer takes a quick screenshot of the success screen and uploads it on your checkout page right before placing their order.

3. How You Will Get Notified

You won't miss a single sale or customer question!

  • New Orders: Every time a customer places an order, you will instantly receive an email at Kondapallisirihamsini9@gmail.com. This email contains the customer's delivery address, a list of what they bought, and the total price.
  • Custom Designs: If a customer asks for a "Custom Design" and uploads inspiration pictures, all of their contact details and images will also be emailed directly to your inbox so you can chat with them.

4. The Admin Portal (Managing Your Shop)

At the very bottom of your website footer under "Quick Links", there is an inconspicuous link called Admin Portal.

  • If you log in with your Admin email (admin@varnamaesthetics.com) and your secret password (password), you unlock special powers!
  • You can easily view all the recent orders that have come in.
  • You can check the payment screenshots to verify that you received the money.
  • Adding Items: You can instantly add new earrings or products to your shop. Just type the product name, set a price, upload a picture, and it immediately appears on your public website!
  • You can delete old products whenever you run out of stock.
